Arknights: Endfield Rotation Builder

Plan Arknights: Endfield team rotations with operator skills, combo triggers, elemental reactions, buffs, debuffs, cooldowns and stack interactions. The tool is built for testing combat timelines, comparing team ideas and sharing repeatable rotations with other players.

Use the planner to build a four-operator team, arrange the skill order, inspect reaction outcomes and track important effects such as Vulnerable, Stagger, Combustion, Solidification, Electrification, Corrosion and Arts Susceptibility.

Arknights: Endfield rotation planner FAQ

What is this rotation builder for?

It helps players test Endfield team rotations, skill timing, combo conditions and reaction chains before sharing or refining a setup.

Which combat details can be tracked?

The planner tracks operator skills, cooldowns, energy, buffs, debuffs, stacks, elemental reactions and selected enemy targets.

Can rotations be shared?

Yes. The tool supports shareable rotation data and community rotation workflows for comparing team ideas.
